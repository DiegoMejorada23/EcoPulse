About EcoPulse

EcoPulse is a community-driven platform designed to empower citizens in monitoring their local environment. By reporting issues like illegal dumping, noise pollution, and infrastructure damage, you help create a data-driven map for advocacy and action.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between community awareness and environmental accountability through transparent, real-time data visualization.
